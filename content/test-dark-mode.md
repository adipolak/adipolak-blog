# Testing Dark Mode

Look for the floating button in the **bottom-right corner** of the page.

- It should have a moon icon 🌙 (or sun ☀️ if already in dark mode)
- Click it to toggle between light and dark modes
- Your preference will be saved

## What to look for:

1. **Button location**: Bottom-right corner, floating above content
2. **Button style**: Purple circular button with emoji
3. **Hover effect**: Should scale up slightly when you hover
4. **Toggle effect**: Background and text colors should change smoothly

If you don't see it, open your browser's developer console (F12) and look for any error messages.
